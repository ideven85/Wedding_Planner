Love Story

We met for the first time

20th June 2016

Cami likes saying she went uninvited to Aqui's birthday party. Partly true, partly fantasy, the fact is that we meet in Amsterdam during a party.

Our first date

5th August 2016

Our first date was in the On the Roof! We went to listen to the Franz Von Chossy Quintet, we talked lots and we finished the evening in a cocktail bar.

We live together

1st December 2016

Aqui fell in parachute in Cami's tiny apartment in Leiden, and she welcomed him with love. After a couple of months they moved to a bigger apartment in Amsterdam, where they live happily.

We decided to get married

4th September 2018

A couple of months before we were talking about it, but we could not decide, it was such a big step. However, one afternoon in the south of Italy we were walking and looking for a medieval bridge, without realising the romantic atmosphere of Calabria surrounded us, and we could not stop saying I love you to each other.
